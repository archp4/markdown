# Masala Khcidi
Take Cooker
Add Oil
Mustart Seed
Cumin Seed
Mix - 30 Seconds

Add following
2 Cloves
3 Black Peppercorns
Some Cinnamon Stick
2 Dry Red Chilli
Mix - 30 Seconds

Add Onion Sliced (2)
Mix Gracli Green Chilli Paste
Mix till Onion Transplant

Add Peanuts
Add Vegs (Green Peas, Carrot, Toamato, Potatoes, etc )
Mix 2 min

Turmeric Powder - 1 / 2 tsp
Red Chilli Powder - 1 tsp
Cumin Powder - 1 / 2 tsp
Coriander Powder - 1 tsp
Garam Masla - 1 tsp
Salt - 1 tsp
Mix on Low Flame until all properly


Mix Rice-Dal
Double Water of Rice & Dal
Mix

3 Whistle 

Ready...
